SUNDOWN RUN — WORLD MAP

Drawn from the real game code · 2026-07-12 · regenerate: bun scripts/world-map.ts

This whole 2 km × 2 km world is grown from code - there is no hand-drawn level file. A few hundred lines in src/core/terrain.ts decide where the hills, the road, the jumps and the banked corners go, and everything else (physics, grass, trees, shadows) reads from that one source of truth.

HOW THE WORLD GETS MADE (in order)

  1. Rolling hills from layered noise - getBaseHeight()
  2. A mountain rim traps the valley - rimHeightAt()
  3. The road is drawn as 44 points, smoothed into a 3.9 km loop - CIRCUIT
  4. The ground is carved flat along it - getTerrainHeight()
  5. Jumps rise out of the road itself - JUMPS
  6. Three corners get tilted like velodromes - BANKS
  7. Playground toys are sculpted from smooth bumps - PLAYGROUNDS
  8. Trees, rocks, grass, props and shards scatter on top - src/world/

The physics collider samples the exact same heights the meshes are drawn from - the car lands on what you see.
